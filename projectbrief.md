## 1. The Mission & Goal
[cite_start]The goal is to transform a traditional Ayurvedic clinic in Brickfields, KL, into a high-performance digital brand[cite: 125, 258]. [cite_start]We are moving away from manual WhatsApp bookings and "PM" culture toward an automated, software-driven "Digital Ecosystem" that handles sales, scheduling, and medical authority 24/7[cite: 259, 273].

## 2. Core Architecture (The Hybrid Stack)
[cite_start]We are building an **Enterprise Hybrid Stack** to ensure sub-second speeds and bank-grade security[cite: 261, 268]:
* [cite_start]**Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router) for SEO and performance[cite: 262].
* [cite_start]**Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) for secure customer and order data[cite: 263].
* [cite_start]**CMS:** Sanity.io for staff-managed blogs, FAQs, and treatments[cite: 264].
* [cite_start]**Booking:** Cal.com integration for real-time doctor scheduling[cite: 265].

## 3. The 4-Hub System (What We Are Building)

### [cite_start]A. The Public Storefront [cite: 274]
* [cite_start]**Premium UX:** A luxury wellness aesthetic using Deep Herbal Green (#2F5D50) and Turmeric Gold (#D4A373)[cite: 143, 145, 151].
* [cite_start]**Smart Combo Engine:** Logic to sell product "Bundles" that auto-updates individual SKU inventory[cite: 276, 277].
* [cite_start]**Authority Content:** A blog engine for SEO-optimized Ayurvedic articles[cite: 283].

### [cite_start]B. The Customer Portal (Client Hub) [cite: 291]
* [cite_start]**Secure Dashboard:** Clients can log in to track orders (Processing -> Shipped)[cite: 292, 293].
* [cite_start]**Appointment Manager:** A private area to view, reschedule, or cancel treatments[cite: 294].

### [cite_start]C. The Admin Command Center [cite: 295]
* [cite_start]**No-Code Control:** A backend for staff to manage inventory, print invoices, and update content without touching code[cite: 296, 297, 298].
* [cite_start]**Automated Comms:** System-generated emails for order confirmations and appointment reminders[cite: 299].

### [cite_start]D. Sales Agent Portal (Affiliate System) [cite: 306]
* [cite_start]**TikTok Tracking:** Specialized dashboard for sales agents to track their unique referral links and commissions[cite: 307, 308].
* [cite_start]**Performance Reports:** Automatic calculation of revenue generated per agent[cite: 309].
